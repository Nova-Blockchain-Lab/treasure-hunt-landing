01 / Features

What Treasure Hunt
Does for Your Event

Treasure Hunt turns any venue into an interactive game. Attendees explore your space, scan checkpoints, earn rewards, and compete on live leaderboards — all from their phone browser, no app download required.

Guide Foot Traffic

You decide where checkpoints go. Every checkpoint is a reason to walk across the venue — driving traffic to sponsors, booths, and underexplored areas.

Reward Real Participation

Attendees earn rewards for engaging — not just showing up. Points they can spend in the built-in merch store on hats, socks, and exclusive items.

Plug Into Any Venue

Drop NFC tags or QR codes anywhere. Works on any phone browser with a simple sign-in — attendees are playing in under 30 seconds, no app download required.

Track Everything in Real Time

See which checkpoints are visited, which areas are underexplored, and who your most engaged attendees are. Live analytics for organizers.

02 / How It Works

Five Steps.
Zero Friction.

01

Open the Game

Visit the URL on your phone. Sign in with one tap. No app download, no complex setup. You're playing in 30 seconds.

02

Explore the Venue

Checkpoints are placed strategically across the event. Follow the map, discover hidden spots, and uncover rewards around every corner.

03

Scan to Collect

Tap an NFC tag or scan a QR code. Instant reward. One touch, one point — collected and tracked automatically.

04

Compete on the Leaderboard

Live rankings update in real time. Social challenges and collaborative missions keep the energy high all day.

05

Spend at the Merch Store

Rewards unlock real prizes — socks, hats, exclusive items. The built-in merch store turns points into something attendees take home.

You control the checkpoints, the rewards, and the prizes. We handle the technology.

03 / Use Cases

Where It
Works

Any venue with a crowd becomes an interactive playground. Treasure Hunt adapts to your scale, your audience, and your goals.

Conferences & Summits

Drive booth traffic and keep attendees engaged between sessions. Turn passive badge-scanners into active participants exploring every corner.

Corporate Events

Gamify team building, product launches, and internal summits. Give employees a reason to explore the space and interact with every activation.

Universities & Campuses

Orientation weeks, open days, and campus events come alive. Students explore, compete, and discover spaces they'd never find on their own.

Festivals & Expos

Create an exploration layer across massive venues. Reward attendees for visiting stages, sponsor areas, and hidden experiences throughout the grounds.

Brand Activations

Connect physical marketing campaigns to measurable engagement. Know exactly which touchpoints drove interaction and for how long.

Hackathons

Keep builders energized between coding sessions. Reward participation across workshops, sponsor booths, and social areas with real prizes.

05 / Proven at Scale

Proven at
Two World-Class Events.

462K+ Rewards • 207 Players • 59 Checkpoints

Treasure Hunt was created by NOVA Blockchain Lab and first deployed at the 1st International Blockchain Confluence Conference — a satellite event of the IEEE International Conference on Distributed Ledger Technologies, organized by NOVA University Lisbon (NOVA IMS + Nova SBE) on November 17–18, 2025 in Lisbon, Portugal. Blockchain Confluence became the first conference ever to deploy Treasure Hunt, proving that interactive event engagement can be seamless, measurable, and genuinely fun.

It evolved. Scaled. Crossed borders.

In 2026, Treasure Hunt became the official engagement game at ETHDenver — one of the world's largest Ethereum events. 207 players competed across 59 venue checkpoints, claiming over 462,000 rewards and spending them in the built-in merch store.
