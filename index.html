01 / What It Is

Not Another
Gamification App

Treasure Hunt is an on-chain, gamified experience that turns conferences, festivals, campuses and corporate environments into interactive blockchain-powered adventures. Participants connect their crypto wallet and explore the venue searching for NFC tags or QR codes. Each discovery unlocks token rewards, challenges, and leaderboard progression — all transparently recorded on-chain.

Wallet-Native Interaction

Every participant connects their own Web3 wallet. Self-custody compatible. Real blockchain transactions from the first tap.

Tokenized Rewards

Real token minting and transfers. Not fake points in a database. Verifiable on-chain reward distribution that participants actually own.

Physical-to-Digital Bridge

NFC tags and QR codes placed throughout the venue connect physical space to blockchain. Tap a tag, trigger a transaction.

Transparent Competition

Live leaderboard with tamper-proof logic. Rankings computed on-chain. Every position earned, every score verifiable.

02 / How It Works

Five Steps.
Fully On-Chain.

01

Connect Your Wallet

Participants log in using their Web3 wallet. No accounts. No passwords. Just sign a message and you're in.

02

Explore the Venue

Physical checkpoints with NFC tags or QR codes are placed strategically across the event. Every corner becomes an opportunity.

03

Tap or Scan to Claim

When a checkpoint is found, users tap or scan to trigger an on-chain interaction and receive tokens. One touch. One transaction. Verified.

04

Earn & Compete

Tokens accumulate. A live leaderboard tracks rankings in real time. Social challenges and collaborative missions increase engagement.

05

Redeem Rewards

Tokens can be exchanged for prizes, exclusive access, NFTs, or other digital and physical incentives. Your tokens, your choice.

Every action generates a blockchain transaction — creating a verifiable, transparent and gamified participation record.

05 / Origin

Built in Portugal.
Deployed Globally.

From Academic Lab to International Stage

Treasure Hunt was created by NOVA Blockchain Lab for Blockchain Confluence 2025 in Lisbon. A research-grade project born inside one of Portugal's leading universities, built to prove that on-chain engagement at physical events isn't just possible — it's superior.

It evolved. Scaled. Crossed borders.

In 2026, Treasure Hunt became the official Web3 game at ETHDenver — placing Portuguese-built blockchain infrastructure on one of the most visible stages in the global Ethereum ecosystem.
